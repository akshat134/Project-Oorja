PROJECT

Project Oorja seeks to facilitate ownership of affordable solar LED lamps among street vendors to meet their lighting needs & promote savings.

What We Do

Identification of target areas and needs assessment.

Development, communication & training of beneficiaries.

Procurement of raw materials from discerned suppliers.

Manufacturing of lamps, involving our direct beneficiaries.

Marketing & after sales service, managed by our intermediaries.
